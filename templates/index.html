About Us

Building Stronger Communities Since 2021

Modaly Inc. is a community-based non-profit organization that actively empowers low income families in Maryland, USA and also women and children in under-resourced communities in Nigeria, delivering essential services such as developmental skills training, healthcare, and education. Our mission drives us to address and eradicate the roots of suicide, poverty, and violence, thereby fostering more robust, more resilient communities where everyone has the opportunity to flourish.

Since its establishment in 2021 and the creation of its Nigerian branch, Modaly Care Foundation, Modaly has made significant strides in supporting underserved women and children. Through focused initiatives in education, heathcare, skill development and entrepreneurship, the foundation has positively impacted over 200 individuals, guiding them away from the brink of poverty and towards a pathway of self-sufficiency and empowerment. This commitment to lifting community’s highlights Modaly’s integral role in fostering sustainable growth and development.
